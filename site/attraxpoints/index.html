AttraXPoints

Earn, climb, and prepare for what’s coming.

AttraXPoints (AXP) are the reward backbone of Attrax.Bet. They’re designed to reward active players, fuel competition, and unlock future perks — all while keeping things fun and transparent.

We’ll be sharing regular updates as AttraXPoints evolve, but here’s what you can know right now:

How to Earn Points
  • First-time sign up bonus – every new account receives XXX AttraXPoints.
  • Daily login claim – check in every day to collect XXX points.
  • Play to earn – you’ll earn points every time you play.
  • Loss multiplier – losing isn’t all bad — if you lose a game, you get extra points (XXX) as a bonus.
Using Points
AttraXPoints are designed for future rewards and perks within Attrax.Bet.
  • Points can be spent later for various rewards (to be revealed).
  • Holding more points will unlock bigger future airdrops.
  • Points are an on-chain token but have no real-world value — they cannot be bought, sold, or transferred.
Fair Play Only
We want AttraXPoints to stay fair and fun:
  • Any attempt to cheat or manipulate the system will result in permanent disqualification from receiving point-related perks.
  • Once disqualified, you won’t be eligible for rewards, airdrops, or leaderboard placements.
Leaderboard
All points earned go toward the AttraXPoints Leaderboard. The more you play and claim, the higher you’ll rank. Expect recognition, bragging rights, and special surprises for top players.
What’s Next
We’re just getting started. New ways to earn, spend, and compete with AttraXPoints will be revealed over time. Expect constant updates, mystery rewards, and surprises designed to keep the competition exciting.

Stay tuned — the more AttraXPoints you collect, the more valuable they’ll become in the Attrax.Bet ecosystem.
